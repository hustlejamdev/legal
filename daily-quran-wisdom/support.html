Frequently Asked Questions

Is the app free?

Yes. Daily Quran Wisdom is completely free. There are no subscriptions or paywalls.

What is the Sadaqah contribution?

The app offers an optional voluntary contribution (Sadaqah) of $1, $3, or $10 to support development and help spread the app. It is entirely optional and does not unlock any features.

How do I request a refund for a contribution?

Refunds are handled by Apple. Please visit reportaproblem.apple.com to submit a refund request.

How do I change the language?

Open the side menu (top left) and tap Settings. You can switch between English and Turkish.

Why is the same verse showing multiple days?

Verses are selected on a daily cycle. If you see the same verse, try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, contact us.

Does the app work offline?

Yes. Once loaded, the daily verse is available offline. An internet connection is only needed for the initial load.

How do I share a verse?

Tap the Share button below the verse. You can share to any app installed on your device.
